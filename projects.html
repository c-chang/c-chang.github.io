Projects

Below are a few of my favorite coding projects I have completed from my graphics course. Click on the titles to see details! I am in the process of adding other projects as well (non-graphics). Stay tuned!

Minecraft Water Shading

Using OpenGLSL, we built a simple water shader compatible with Optifine 1.14 for Minecraft. We used the Blinn-Phong shading model over time to capture a "reflective" water effect, and incorporated wave movement as well. The GIF shows our final result!

Cloth Simulating

Implemented a mesh grid of springs, which was used to simulate the falling effects of different types of cloth by editing the spring constant factor. Additionally, implemented bump and displacement mapping, and different textures.

Pathtracer I & II

Part I of the project involved setting up the ray-object intersection using a Bounding Volume Hierarchy and calculating incident rays for various types of illumination with both random and importance sampling of light sources.

In Part II of the project, I implemented ray-tracing for illumination of mirror and glass materials, as well as microfacets.
